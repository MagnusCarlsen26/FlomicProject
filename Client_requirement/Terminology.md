---

### Nature of Calls

1.  **New Call (NC)**
    *   **Definition:** First-ever visit to a customer.
    *   **Purpose:** Introduction, understanding customer requirements, and initial engagement.
2.  **Follow-Up Call (FC)**
    *   **Definition:** Meetings with a customer after the New Call but before they are onboarded.
    *   **Purpose:** Progress discussions, clarify requirements, and move the customer towards onboarding.
3.  **Joint Sales Visit (JSV)**
    *   **Definition:** Visit conducted with a senior Team member.
    *   **Timing:** Typically occurs on the 3rd or 4th visit during the Follow-Up phase.
    *   **Purpose:** Strengthen relationship, handle complex queries, and accelerate onboarding.
4.  **Service Call (SC)**
    *   **Definition:** Meetings with a customer after they have been onboarded.
    *   **Purpose:** Address post-onboarding needs, provide support, and maintain the relationship.

> **NOTE:** Nature of call can be **NC, FC, JSV, SC, or In-office**. If a salesperson is not performing a sales visit, they must record the activity as **In-office**.

### Customer Classification

| Classification | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Existing** | Salesperson's currently active customers. |
| **Targeted (Budgeted)** | Target customers specifically mentioned in the salesperson's budget list. |
| **New Customer (Non-Budgeted)** | New customers not previously mentioned in the Budget list. |
